About Us

We are a family-owned business in Lorain, Ohio. We got our start 40 years ago when Betty Sue, our grandmother started selling her homemade popsicles at parks and local summer events. We have grown into a wholesale provider of wholesome summer treats. We use only non-GMO ingredients that you can pronounce. Great for summer camps, retreats, and even family reunions!
