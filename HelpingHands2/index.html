About Us

We are a local, non-profit organization. Our goal is to connect those in need with those who able to give. Use our site to find goods and services you need, or to trade what you no longer need!

Help your community while clearing out the clutter in your life! If you have items you no longer need, offer them on your profile. You can donate them or search for goods or services you need in exchange.
